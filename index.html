Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Since the start of the pandemic, Animal Crossing: New Horizons has become one of the most popular games of 2020.

I moved back home in the middle of March and about a month later in April I bought this game. It's as addictive as reviews say,

and I spent most of this self-quarantine playing it until school started again in September. With the virtual element of the game,

I was also allowed to play with my friends, who I couldn't see in person because of COVID.

Minecraft

The only other video game I have is Minecraft, which I got my brother and I for his birthday in June.

He loves Minecraft and the idea was when I move out we can play with each other on there.

Minecraft has also kept him busy during the pandemic, since he wasn't allowed to go to camp over

the summer or see any of his friends either.
